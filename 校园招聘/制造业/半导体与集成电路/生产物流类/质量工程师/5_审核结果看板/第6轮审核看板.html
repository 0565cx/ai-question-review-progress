本轮题目主要出错点总结

Round 6 question-level issues mainly come from role drift and oral-answerability. A subset of items is mechanical-engineering oriented (transmission design, GD&T symbol explanation) and does not match semiconductor quality engineer scope, triggering A2/B6. A smaller subset requires symbol/logic explanation in the prompt itself, triggering A4. Question-level defects are fewer than answer-level defects but directly lead to rewrite-question decisions.

本轮答案主要出错点总结

The dominant pattern is B1 factual/standard mismatch: SPC rule numbering, Cp/Cpk/Ppk interpretation, JEDEC mapping, IATF clause references, and DOE design-resolution consistency. Most stems are still usable, but answer content is not reliable enough for interview quality control. Therefore, rewrite-answer is the primary downstream action this round.
