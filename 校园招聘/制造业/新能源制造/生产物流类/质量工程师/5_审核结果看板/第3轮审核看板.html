Round 3 question-side issues are concentrated in oral-answerability and semantic duplication. A small number of prompts still ask candidates to write formulas or provide derivations (A4), and some items are near-duplicate variants (C1/C2). 本轮答案主要出错点总结

Answer-side issues are mainly B-type: third-layer scenarios are occasionally too niche for routine quality engineering practice, or answer structure is too dense for spoken responses. Most defects are in second/third layer alignment and role-specific framing rather than core concept accuracy.

后续重新出内容时应补充的规则

【重新出题】
For rewritten questions, ban wording that requests writing formulas, derivations, or diagram drawing. Keep each item focused on one core competency in common quality-engineering contexts, and enforce anti-duplication checks before finalizing.

【重出答案】
For rewritten answers, enforce strict 3-layer alignment with the question scope: Layer1 concept correctness, Layer2 2-3 practical options with trade-offs, Layer3 one high-frequency exception plus one high-frequency complex case with actionable steps. Exclude niche or cross-role recommendations.
