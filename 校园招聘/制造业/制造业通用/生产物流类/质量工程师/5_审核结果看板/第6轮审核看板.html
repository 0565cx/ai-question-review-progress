本轮答案主要出错点总结

Round 6 answer issues are concentrated in B1 factual errors. The most frequent problems are incorrect GB/T 2828.1 switching rules, confusing normal-to-tightened and normal-to-reduced inspection, wrong Ac/Re acceptance logic, incorrect AQL risk interpretation, and inaccurate APQP, PPAP, Ppk/Cpk, SPC, or IATF references. These errors directly affect the quality engineer scoring standard, so most problematic items should keep the question and regenerate the answer.

后续重新出内容时应补充的规则

【重新出题】
When regenerating questions, explicitly provide inspection state, consecutive accepted or rejected batch history, sample size, Ac/Re, AQL, defect class, customer requirements, and whether substitutions are allowed. Do not combine contradictory conditions or mix standard switching rules with customer-internal recheck rules.

【重出答案】
When regenerating answers, verify GB/T 2828.1 switching rules and acceptance logic first: d <= Ac means accept and d >= Re means reject; tightened inspection is not triggered by a single rejected batch; reduced inspection is not triggered by only five accepted batches; tightened inspection cannot directly switch to reduced inspection. Also verify APQP, PPAP, Ppk/Cpk, SPC, and IATF references before writing the three answer layers.
